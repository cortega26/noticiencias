## What Was Done in the Previous Session

### Problem
Four articles were missing their hero images on the front-end. Three had no `image` field in their frontmatter; one had the field but pointed to a non-existent file.

Additionally, article `2026-03-27-el-error-de-redondeo...` had a leaked LLM meta-instruction preamble ("Aquí tienes el artículo, redactado con el enfoque de Editor Científico Senior en Noticiencias:") at the top of its body — the same issue the Codex bot flagged in a PR that could not merge because CI/CD is offline (credit card issue).

### Confirmed Root Cause

## Architecture Notes

- Hero images are rendered in `src/layouts/PostLayout.astro` — conditional `{imageSrc && (...)}`, no fallback
- Local images: `~/assets/images/` → resolved via `import.meta.glob` in `src/utils/images.ts`
- Public images: `/images/` → static files in `public/images/`
- Content schema: `src/content/config.ts` — `image` is `z.union([z.string(), z.object({...})]).optional()`
- The new guardrail `scripts/check-hero-images.js` validates presence and file existence for local paths
